Student Grants

A  number of student grants for participating at DL 2026 is available for students with limited funding.

The grants consist of a fixed amount that can be used to support with registration fees and/or travel costs.

Grants will be given to motivated students who are genuinely interested in attending the workshop and lack sufficient funding.

Preference will be given to students who have a paper (or extended abstract) accepted at DL 2026.

Applications for student grants can be submitted using the DL 2026 <a href="https://forms.gle/TUaZXU2zjB4jX4j28">Student Grant Application Google form</a>

<h4>Important Dates:</h4>
 - May 22nd Student grants application deadline
 - May 27th Student grant notification
